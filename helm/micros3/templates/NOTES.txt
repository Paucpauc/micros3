MicroS3 has been deployed!

S3 API is available through the leader service:
  export NODE_IP=$(kubectl get nodes --output wide --no-headers | head -1 | awk '{print $6}')
  aws --endpoint-url=http://$NODE_IP:{{ .Values.service.leader.s3.nodePort }} s3 ls

Port-forward for direct access:
  kubectl port-forward statefulset/{{ include "micros3.fullname" . }} 9000:{{ .Values.service.headless.s3.port }}

Health check:
  curl http://$NODE_IP:{{ .Values.service.leader.s3.nodePort }}/health

Metrics:
  curl http://$NODE_IP:{{ .Values.service.leader.s3.nodePort }}/metrics
